# Netflix_Data_analysis
Netflix Data Analysis

Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze Netflix content trends and understand how the platformâ€™s catalog has evolved over time in terms of content type, country of production, genre distribution, and release patterns.

---

Tools Used

- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Matplotlib & Seaborn
- Google Sheets (Interactive Dashboard)

---

Dataset

Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset containing information about title, type, country, release year, duration, genre, and directors.

---

Analysis Performed

- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Handling missing values
- Content type distribution (Movies vs TV Shows)
- Country-wise content production
- Year-wise content growth
- Genre frequency analysis
- Director contribution analysis

---

Key Insights

- Netflix prioritizes movies over TV shows, focusing on quick-consumption entertainment.
- Major content growth occurred after 2016 during global expansion.
- The United States dominates production, but international content is increasing.
- Drama and International genres are the most common.
- A small group of directors contributes a large portion of titles.
